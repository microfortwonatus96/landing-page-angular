What is Rantai?

Rantai is a "Dual Cloud" based service to facilitate management and improve the efficiency of distribution companies. Ranging from purchasing, selling, inventory, accounts receivable, POS to business intelligence for company management. Rantai automatically capture and extract sales order data, such as a customer’s past purchases from the company, as well as unstructured data such as written communications and saves it to an electronic archive.


Rantai helps organize and manage business easily through one integrated platform to various e-commerce sales channels in Indonesia. It’s Keep your business growing at the dramatic rates you plan for and integrated around a single codebase, database and business process. Which can be initiated from anywhere, using any computer, tablet, or smartphone.
